Welcome to ROK COVID19 Information center

This is the web for the desktop

The following are provided.

  • Overview

    • We monitor the overall coivd-19 situation in Korea. The Infected counter shows the number of people who can actually spread the virus. The total recovered counter represents the number of people who have completed the accumulated healing. The total died counter represents the cumulative number of deaths.

  • SIR Prediction

    • It provides prediction of corona trends using SIR models from present to up to 100 days. You can check the current number of patients and the total number of cured patients.

  • COVID19 News

    • You can check the latest corona-related news released by the Korean media.
